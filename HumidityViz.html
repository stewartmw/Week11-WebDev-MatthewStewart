Humidity vs. Latitude




The scatter plot shown here illustrates humidity vs. latitude. From the scatter plot, we can see that there does not appear to be a correlation between latitude and humidity. One interesting observation, however, is that there appear to be a cluster of cities between latitudes -20 and 20 that have 100% humidity. This makes sense given that the world's tropical rainforests lie between the Tropic of Capricorn (23.5 degrees south) and the Tropic of Cancer (23.5 degrees north).
